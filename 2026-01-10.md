### Song: [Juicy](https://youtu.be/_JZom_gVfuw?si=5sHkV8Uw2st5wkP3)

### Artist: Notorious B.I.G

### Year Of Production: 1994

### Genre: HipHop

The year is 1994, [Christopher Wallace](https://share.google/Q4DETBmLp8PGF1wqL) is still under the Bad Boy Records music label being lead by Sean Combs popularly known as P-Diddy.
B.I.G still being under Bad Boy Records, it is Diddy and Poke who produced the song.
The song Draws it's beats and instruments majorly from the [Juicy Fruit](https://youtu.be/EYE5QEBOMA8?si=l6G0gd5yKR8ej9sk) by Mtume.
The songs theme is about Biggies transition from Rags to Riches despite being a Black.
Biggie is celebrating how he moved from being poor to being able to own luxurious and expensive things a sign and status of wealth and Riches to him.

In the song's intro, despite being a bright student as seen from the grades he got, he views his education as something that never materialised after all for him.

In the songs Chorus, there are female voices which are [Total](https://share.google/pyfIn2lqtOGfvziMp) music group which was by the time signed to the Bad Boys Records.

To even help his listeners understand his background more, he raps about eating sardines for dinner to owning Limousines.

He also asserts his wealth status when he says, _...and if you dont know now you know_ away to remind people of where he was from to where he is currently at.

Despite experiencing all that level of success, he still doesnt forget his roots and even goes ahead to name his crib the same number and hood. _...call the crib same number same hood_.

He also raps about blowing up like world trade which is in direct relationship to the WTC but metaphorically to show how much of a success he has achieved despite being from a poor background.
