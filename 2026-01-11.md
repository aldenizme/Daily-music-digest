### Song: [7 Years](https://youtu.be/LHCob76kigA?si=YGUMCksdeTKmb36M)

### Artist: Lukas Graham

### Year of Production: 2015

#### Genre: Pop

The year is 2015, the popular Danish band [Lukas Graham](https://share.google/cjWzdCo6ptTf8ufen) just released their 7 years song which is a story narration by [Lukas Forchhammer](https://share.google/iKzPsgxOCdw0ZBWpN). The song is about Reflection on life, Passage of time, mortality and Relationships as the founadtional instrumenents to humans life.
When the song is released, it gains a lot of vieweship and the band experinces a lot of accolades and success. The song even earned multiple grammy award nominations.

Lukas Graham narrates on how he got advice from his mother when he was young on how he needs to make friends as in the introduction to the song. He later talks about how he even got more advice on the importance of having a family and children lest he becomes lonely.
He also talks about his adult life abusing drugs with friends as he tends to follow his dreams and the advices that he got from his parents. He starts writing songs as a way to fulfill his inner desires and dreams.

He remembers his fathers mortality at 61 years and those of his friends which even makes him stop his song narration at 60 years. He contemplates how his old age will be like dreaming of making a good father to his children so that they can be able to visit him once or twice a month.

The song was inspired by his own life story and experiences as heard from the introduction lyrics on his young age to mid age to later 30's.

It is important to note that unlike most pop songs with choruses and hooks, this song is more about narration, zero traditional choruses and hook.
