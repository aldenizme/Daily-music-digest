### Artist: Black Eyed Peas

### Year of Production: 2011

### Genre: Pop rap

Shortly before the earthquake(A week prior), [Black Eyed Peas](https://share.google/msGjPvM0OO00ha0OC) an American Musical group just finished filming the popular Just cant get enough song there.
The song features 4 of the members of the group, Fergie, Will.i.am, Apl.de.ap and Taboo.
The band becaame famous in the 2000's due to their blend of Hiphop, Dance, Pop, Electronic music and the use of Auto Tune to enhance their magical voices.

The song was released as part of their [Beginning Album](https://open.spotify.com/album/3IpexNXiKbezuY526S4Jvy?si=jFlgUBBBSomJ2TTwoQjc7Q).
The song is a compilation of lyrics that are metaphorical in trying to explain the addiction and Euphoria that comes with Love.
The central theme in the song is Intense Desire, Romance and Emotional dependency.
Due to the songs popularity and reach, it once held top 3 in the Billboard Hot 100.
The song has a dedication for the Japaneasse people at it's start to offer emotional support to them.
On the surface, the song depicts how someone can hopelessly be in love while Taboo even gives some other agenda that fueled the music which is the loneliness and separation that travelling musicians encounter when they are on their tours away from famillies and loved ones.

This is one of the song that blends duality of love in the essence of Intensity and vulnerability.
